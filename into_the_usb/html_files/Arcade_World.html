Arcade World

ARTIST: Bastien

COLLECTION: bastien-editions

EDITION: 1/1

DESCRIPTION
Welcome to the Arcade world, your after-school haven of neon dreams. Lose yourself in the maze of lights and sounds, leaving responsibilities behind. Kids run around playing games and goofing off. Schoolmates, friends, crushes, and enemies all come in peace, ready to spend their pocket money. After a long school day, this blissful experience is yours to enjoy for as long as you can.

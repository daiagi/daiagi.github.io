Silent Night, A Degen's Lair

ARTIST: Bastien

COLLECTION: Holders drop

EDITION: 1/1

DESCRIPTION
It’s late at night and you’re in your room, surrounded by darkness and the light of your TV. Your thoughts race about NFTs as you trade and watch the markets. You read technology magazines and joke about never taking profits. Empty energy drink cans clutter your room as you watch news about new projects and dream of wealth. This is your life now - trading, watching, joking, and fading.
